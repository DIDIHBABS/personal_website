Curious, data-driven and emerging tech enthusiast.

I'm Rhoda Babatunde, a Machine Learning Developer with over 15 years of hands-on experience in User Experience, Product Analytics and Strategic Thinking.

My journey into Artificial Intelligence ↗ began at the tender age of 12, when I found myself reporting bugs in the MS-DOS operating system to programmers during my first part-time job. By 16, as a self-taught, I had already created my first website. That's how I discovered this was a craft, and I decided to pursue it.

Throughout my career, I have worn many hats, collaborating with teams from various countries and across a wide range of sectors, including SaaS, PaaS, and DSaaS. Driven by data and a passion for emerging tech, I excel at adapting swiftly to new challenges, aiming to ensure that the designs lead in innovation and effectiveness.

For a more in-depth understanding of my qualifications, you can follow or connect with me on LinkedIn ↗.
