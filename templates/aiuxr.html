Over the past 15 years, my work in the UX community has been recognized in countries such as France, Ireland, Spain, the USA, and Brazil, a testament to my effort and dedication. I also actively engage with peers by publishing articles and sharing my insights through speaking engagements, workshops, and interviews in other countries, including the UK, Canada, Slovenia, and Poland.

I spread my enthusiasm for designing by sharing my expertise with budding UX professionals worldwide as a UX Mentor CareerFoundry ↗, a top-notch EdTech company based in Germany. My mentees have rated their mentoring experience ★ 4.97 out of 5 stars across several criteria, including helpfulness and guidance. I'm also part of Toptal ↗, a global network of the top 3% of freelance talent, with a proven track record and elite industry experience. This position is a testament to my ability to navigate the global market—a skill I'm eager to share with you.
